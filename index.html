About the Center

A sanctuary for spiritual growth and technological excellence

Our Mission

To provide a peaceful environment for Buddhist practice while leveraging modern technology to spread the Dhamma to the global community. We believe in the harmonious integration of spiritual development and digital innovation.

Our Vision

To become a leading digital platform for Buddhist education, connecting practitioners worldwide through thoughtfully designed web experiences that embody mindfulness, clarity, and compassion in every pixel.

Our Journey

2015

Foundation

Weluwanarama was established as a small meditation center with a vision to serve the local community.

2018

Digital Expansion

Launched our first website to reach global practitioners, offering online Dhamma talks and meditation guides.

2021

Web Development Integration

Combined Buddhist teachings with professional web development services to create meaningful digital experiences.

2024

International Recognition

Recognized as a leading Buddhist center with award-winning web presence and educational platform.
